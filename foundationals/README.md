# GTME Foundationals

The thinking behind the tools.

GTM Soma covers the what — which tools to use, how to set them up, how they connect. Foundationals covers the why — the creators whose frameworks and mental models shaped modern GTM engineering.

Each writeup covers: who they are, their core thesis, the frameworks they use (with concrete examples), their toolstack, where to start learning from them, and what I take from their work vs. where I do it differently.
